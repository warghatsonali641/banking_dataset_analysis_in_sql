# 🏦 Banking Data Analysis (SQL Project)

## 📌 Overview

This project focuses on **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** using SQL on a banking dataset.
The objective is to understand customer behavior, financial activity, and relationships between accounts, loans, transactions, and credit cards.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset consists of multiple CSV files representing different banking operations:

* Customers – basic customer details
* Accounts – account type, balance, and status
* Transactions – transaction records
* Loans – loan details including interest rates and status
* Credit Cards – credit card usage data
* ATMs – ATM-related informations

### 📊 Accounts

* Distribution of account types (Savings, Checking, Investment)
* Account balances and activity status
* Trends in account openings

### 💳 Transactions

* Customer transaction patterns
* High-value and frequent transactions
* Spending behavior insights

### 🏦 Loans

* Loan types such as Mortgage and Auto Loans
* Active vs Closed loans
* Loan durations and repayment patterns

### 👥 Customers

* Customer segmentation
* Relationship between customers and accounts
* Financial behavior analysis

### 💡 Credit Cards

* Credit usage patterns
* Utilization analysis

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Savings accounts are the most commonly used
* Mortgage loans have higher values compared to other loans
* Most accounts are active
* Customers often hold multiple financial products
